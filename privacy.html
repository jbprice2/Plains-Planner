Plains Planner

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2025

Not affiliated with Auburn University. The Plains Planner is an independent student-built project. It is not endorsed by, sponsored by, or connected to Auburn University in any official capacity.

Who We Are

The Plains Planner ("we," "us," or "the app") is an independent web application built by an Auburn University student to help Auburn students organize their clubs, events, schedules, and involvement hours. The app is accessible at jbprice2.github.io/Plains-Planner.

Questions about this policy can be directed to: jbprice2@icloud.com

What Information We Collect

We only collect what you put into the app:

  • Account email address — if you choose to sign in (via magic link or Google). Anonymous users have no email on file.
  • App content you create — clubs you add, calendar events, recurring schedule items, and portfolio/activity log entries.

We do not collect your Auburn credentials, your Student ID, your GPA, or any data from Auburn University systems.

How We Use Your Information

  • To sync your data across devices when you are signed in.
  • To restore your data when you return to the app.

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share your data with third parties except as described below.

Data Storage and Third-Party Services

Your data is stored in Supabase, a cloud database service. Supabase stores your data on secured servers and is governed by its own privacy policy.

If you sign in with Google, your authentication is handled by Google's OAuth service. We receive only your email address from Google — nothing else from your Google account.

The app is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect standard web server logs (IP address, browser, page visits) as part of hosting. See GitHub's privacy statement.

Anonymous Use

You can use The Plains Planner without creating an account. When you do, a temporary anonymous session is created in your browser. Your data is stored under that anonymous session in our database. If you clear your browser data or switch devices without signing in, you will lose access to that anonymous session's data.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account exists or as long as your anonymous session is active. You may delete all of your data at any time by using the export/import tool to back up your data, then contacting us at jbprice2@icloud.com to request account deletion.

We will honor deletion requests within a reasonable time.

Cookies and Local Storage

The app uses browser localStorage to store your session token so you stay logged in between visits. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

Children's Privacy

The Plains Planner is intended for college students (18 and older). We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13. If you believe a minor has submitted information to us, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For any privacy questions or data deletion requests, email jbprice2@icloud.com.
